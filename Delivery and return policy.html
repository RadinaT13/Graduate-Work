At Reckless Necklace, we strive to provide a seamless and satisfactory shopping experience for our valued customers. To ensure transparency and customer satisfaction, we have established the following delivery and return policy:

Delivery

1. Shipping Options

We offer various shipping options to cater to your preferences. During the checkout process, you can select the shipping method that best suits your needs.

2. Processing Time

Once your order is placed, we make every effort to process and dispatch it as quickly as possible. Please allow us 7-10 business days for order processing before shipment.

3. Shipment Tracking

For your convenience, we provide tracking information for all shipped orders. You can track your package's journey from our store to your doorstep.

 

Return policy

Satisfaction Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If, for any reason, you are not satisfied with your order, please contact our customer support within 14 days of receiving the product.

Eligibility

To be eligible for a return or exchange, the item must be in its original condition, unworn, and free from any signs of damage or alteration.

Return Process

To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer support team with your order details. We will guide you through the return process and provide you with a return shipping label if applicable.

Refunds and Exchanges

Once we receive the returned item, we will inspect it to ensure it meets the eligibility criteria. If approved, we will process your refund or exchange in a timely manner, according to our policies.

Return Shipping Costs

Please note that return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a product defect or an error on our part.

 

Important Note

For hygiene and safety reasons, certain jewelry items, such as earrings, may not be eligible for return or exchange unless they are defective or damaged upon arrival.

Please refer to our detailed Terms and Conditions for more information regarding our delivery and return policy. If you have any questions or require further assistance, our friendly customer support team is ready to help.
